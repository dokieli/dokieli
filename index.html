Create, connect and share your ideas

dokieli is a clientside editor for decentralised article publishing, annotations, and social interactions.

Highlight

Dokieli promotes self-publishing by enabling users to publish and annotate articles using their preferred identities and personal storage, supporting both personal and social activities. Built as an open-source project using open web standards, any content created with dokieli can be used in other standards-conforming applications.
